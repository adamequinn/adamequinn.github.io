Adam Quinn

About

Adam Quinn received his Ph.D. in Social Work and an M.S. in Statistics from the University of Georgia. He received his Master of Social Work (M.S.W.) from the University of Washington in Seattle. He was born in Dayton, Ohio.

His research interests include investigating social determinants of health and economic disparities, examining predictors of secondary traumatic stress among social workers and mental health professionals, and understanding factors related to alcohol-use disorders among older adults across the life course.

He lives in Portland, Maine (adamq.yt[at]gmail[dot]com)
